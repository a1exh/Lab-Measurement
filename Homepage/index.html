Development on this version of Lab::VISA has stopped, mainly because of a certain frustration with NI-VISA Linux support. The replacement and successor is called Lab::Measurement and has a new separate homepage. Although it is at the moment still in beta stage, we already use it in the lab in real measurement applications.

Authors

The Lab::VISA system was originally developed by Daniel Schröer and then continued by Andreas K. Hüttel, Daniela Taubert, and Daniel Schröer. Most of the documentation was written by Daniel Schröer.
