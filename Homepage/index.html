The Perl packages Lab::VISA, Lab::Instrument, and Lab::Tools offer instrument and measurement control with Perl via National Instrument's VISA library. Encapsulating the complexity of VISA calls into a straightforward to use library, they are designed to make any lab power user happy.

The software packages can be downloaded from CPAN: just follow the links for Lab::VISA, Lab::Instrument, and Lab::Tools.

Although they are already used by at least two research groups in ongoing measurements, these packages are still very much work in progress. Please bear with us while we improve code and documentation.

Otherwise, you can have a look at the documentation (this is the trunk development version with features soon to come :o), and also subscribe to our lab-visa-users mailing list. This mailing list is also the right place to give feedback and ask for help.

Our `reference customers' so far are :)

  • nanophysics group, Prof. J. P. Kotthaus, LMU München
